LHFV in Articia

Date: 1st July 2026 | Location: Articia

There have been outbreaks of LHFV previously Articia, but these were small outbreaks confined to the forest. Now, it has reached some of Articia's major cities. Some preliminary epidmiological outbreak investigations have been completed

Key facts

  • There has already been 7 cases in Rachelburg, a city of 5 million people
  • Serial interval, mean = 13 days, standard deviation = 4.5 days
